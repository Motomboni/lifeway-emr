# Modern EMR
## Better Care. Faster Decisions. Stronger Revenue Control.

---

### Designed for modern clinics that want to scale without chaos

Modern EMR is a role-based, visit-centered digital platform that helps healthcare teams move from fragmented paperwork and delays to coordinated, auditable, and revenue-protected care.

From registration to consultation, diagnostics, pharmacy, billing, and closure, every step is connected in one secure workflow.

---

## Why Clinics Choose Modern EMR

- **Faster patient flow** with streamlined, visit-scoped workflows
- **Clear team accountability** through strict role-based access
- **Revenue protection** with billing controls and payment enforcement
- **Clinical confidence** with complete patient history and integrated records
- **Compliance readiness** through detailed audit trails

---

## End-to-End Care Workflow

Modern EMR enforces a structured care journey:

`Visit -> Consultation -> Lab/Radiology -> Prescription -> Billing -> Closure`

This prevents process gaps, reduces clinical risk, and improves service quality across departments.

---

## Core Modules

### 1) Patient and Front Desk Operations
- Digital patient registration and search
- Visit creation and queue management
- Appointment scheduling support
- Role-specific access for reception teams

### 2) Consultation Workspace
- Single-screen consultation interface for doctors
- Structured clinical documentation
- Clear handoff to labs, radiology, and pharmacy

### 3) Diagnostics and Pharmacy
- Lab order and result management
- Radiology order and report workflows
- Prescription authoring and dispensing controls
- Visibility of clinical outputs inside visit context

### 4) Billing and Payments
- Multi-method payment support (Cash, POS, Transfer, Wallet, Paystack)
- Pending billing queue for receptionist operations
- End-of-day reconciliation tools
- Outstanding balance visibility and controls

### 5) Management, Compliance, and Reporting
- Comprehensive audit logging (who did what, when)
- Health monitoring endpoints
- Export and print support
- Analytics/reporting capabilities
